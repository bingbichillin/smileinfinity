Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our customer service line for immediate assistance.

What languages do you support?

Our customer service team provides support in Japanese and English. We also have staff members who can assist in Korean and Chinese for basic inquiries.

Can I visit your office in person?

Yes, we welcome office visits by appointment. Please call ahead to schedule a meeting with our team. Our office is located in Shibuya, Tokyo, with easy access from major train stations.

Do you offer emergency support outside business hours?

Yes, our emergency hotline is available 24/7 for urgent childcare situations and safety concerns. For non-emergency inquiries, please use our regular contact methods.
