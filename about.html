About KidsCare Japan

Revolutionizing childcare in Japan through innovative technology and rigorous safety standards

Our Story

Founded in 2020, KidsCare Japan emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create the safest, most reliable babysitting platform in Japan. Our founders, parents themselves, understood the challenges of finding trustworthy childcare in today's busy world.

We recognized that traditional babysitting services lacked the transparency and security that modern families deserve. That's why we developed our innovative platform that combines cutting-edge technology with rigorous safety protocols.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of families across Japan, connecting them with verified, professional babysitters who share our commitment to child safety and exceptional care.

Uncompromising Security

Every babysitter on our platform undergoes extensive background checks and verification processes to ensure your family's safety.

Reference Checks

Ongoing Monitoring
