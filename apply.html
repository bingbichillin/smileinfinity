Join Our Team

Become a trusted babysitter with KidsCare Japan and make a meaningful difference in families' lives while building a rewarding career in childcare.

Our comprehensive application process ensures we select only the most qualified and trustworthy babysitters for our families.

Application Requirements

To ensure the highest standards of childcare, all applicants must meet the following requirements and provide the necessary documentation.

Basic Requirements

Age Requirement

Must be at least 18 years old

Legal Status

Legal right to work in Japan (Japanese citizen, permanent resident, or valid work visa)

Language Skills

Conversational Japanese and/or English proficiency

Experience

Minimum 1 year of childcare experience (professional or personal)

Availability

Flexible schedule with availability for evenings and weekends

Preferred Qualifications

Education Background

Degree in Early Childhood Education, Child Development, or related field

Professional Certifications

Childcare license, teaching certificate, or nursing qualification

Safety Certifications

Current CPR and First Aid certification

Special Skills

Musical instruments, sports coaching, tutoring abilities, or special needs experience

Transportation

Valid driver's license and access to reliable transportation

What Happens Next?

Application Review (1-5 days)

Our team will carefully review your application and verify your qualifications.

  • • Initial application screening
  • • Document verification
  • • Reference contact
  • • Background check initiation

Interview Process (1-2 weeks)

If your application is approved, we'll schedule interviews and assessments.

  • • Phone/video interview
  • • In-person interview
  • • Skills assessment
  • • Safety training session

Final Approval (1 week)

Complete final steps and receive approval to start working.

  • • Background check completion
  • • Contract signing
  • • Platform training
  • • Profile creation
